Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery times?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery (1-2 days) and Next Day delivery options are also available.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes! We offer free standard delivery on all orders over £50 within the UK mainland.

Can I return my order?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products in their original packaging.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for businesses. Visit our wholesale page for more information.

How should I store the dates?

Store in a cool, dry place. Once opened, transfer to an airtight container. Can be refrigerated for extended freshness.

Are your dates organic?

Our premium Medjool dates are 100% natural with no added sugar or preservatives. We also offer certified organic options.
