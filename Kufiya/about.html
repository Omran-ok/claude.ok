About Kufiya Dates

Bringing the finest Jordanian Medjool dates to the United Kingdom since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Kufiya Dates began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of premium Jordanian Medjool dates to families across the United Kingdom.

As part of Farah Import & Export Ltd., we have established strong relationships with the finest date farms in Jordan, ensuring that every box of Kufiya Dates represents the highest quality and authentic Middle Eastern heritage.

Our founder, Ahmad Farah, grew up in Jordan surrounded by date palms. When he moved to the UK, he noticed the lack of truly premium dates in the market. This inspired him to create Kufiya Dates – a bridge between the ancient date cultivation traditions of Jordan and the discerning UK market.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across the UK, from individual families to restaurants and retailers, all seeking the unmatched quality of genuine Jordanian dates.

Date palms in the Jordan Valley where our dates are grown

Why "Kufiya"?

كوفية

The name "Kufiya" represents the traditional Palestinian scarf, symbolizing heritage, authenticity, and cultural pride. Just as the kufiya represents Palestinian identity, our dates represent the authentic taste and quality of Jordan.

Each date is a connection to the rich agricultural traditions of the Jordan Valley, where perfect climate conditions and centuries of expertise create the world's finest Medjool dates.
